2025-03-16

All my life felt not quite right
I tried and tried, lost in the night
I did not know of what i sought
A nameless fear could not be fought

I just gasped and sat up straight
A revelation, a loss of weight
It took 40 hard years to see
There is a girl inside of me

I want to mourn, to snuff the flame
Perhaps I can restart this game
A full life where i can be
No more him, but instead me

I still have time in this shell
To craft a heaven; to play my bell
So i will stretch into my skin
For my real life can now begin
