--- title: Arrival --- Arrival - 2025-03-13

2025-03-13

She was nothing, almost.

A soundless whisper of thought

A dream that hasn’t happened

Yet!

She was not on his radar

He only scanned outside

Feeling his emptiness

She quietly built

She was in the dreams he didn’t remember

He didn’t realise

That gentle hands began to hold warmly from within

She was looking down

At her own hands

He started to fade away

Smiling gently
