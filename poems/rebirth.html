2025-03-16

A shaft of light tears apart the night.
A sonic smash of an awakening cry.
The burning force of repurposed blood.
The crushing weight of awoken dreams.

Two peaks crack violently through the ribcage.
A mound sinks back into the pelvis.
Hips break apart to a wider stance.
Silken tendrils swarm the scalp.

A violent birth, 40 years in a blink.
Vague memories snap into a complete picture.
A name uncovered in the wreckage.
And I watch her climb from the destruction, complete and untouched.
